About

WSAiLab

The lab is coming on-stream in October once we move into the space that is currently in use by MA Communication Design students completing their final major projects. We intend to roll-out slowly in the first term and work towards an official launch in January 2017.

In the Autumn term, we will be providing workshops for MA Communication Design and Year 1 BA Games Design and Art. We look forward to adding more and varied workshops for other courses and other people once things have bedded in.

WSAiLab will be run as a cooperative. We are setting up a steering group that will meet on a regular basis to discuss progress and future initiatives. Operational management of WSAiLab is done by Andy Lapham Senior Teaching Fellow at WSA and the Research Lead is James Branch Senior Teaching Fellow and Course Leader for MA Communications Design at WSA.
