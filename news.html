Understanding Flocking, Crowds and Queues

Tue 22 Nov 2016 : 10:00 - 16:00

Our first workshop looks at algorithms to mimic the behaviour seen in birds flocking and insects swarming. The same algorithms can be used to model human behaviours such as crowds, queuing and fight/battle scenes. More details on the Activities page or email Andy Lapham or just pop in to the ground floor of the South Building at WSA for a chat.
