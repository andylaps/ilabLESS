Welcome to ...

… the WSAiLab website. This is the online presence of the new interactions and prototyping lab at Winchester School of Art, University of Southampton. The lab is coming on-stream in October once we move into the space that is currently in use by MA Communication Design students completing their final major projects. We intend to roll-out slowly in the first term and work towards an official launch in January 2017.

Currently, there’s not a huge amount to see on this website, apart from the start of a resource list, notes on what will go where and the occasional pithy quote. There’s a fair amount of filler text and odd bits of Dylan lyrics. If you're looking for the quotes and lyrics - and why wouldn't you be - note that mostly they don't appear on a mobile screen thanks to the wonders of responsive design. All else said, the structure is all here and working, so it’s a good time to send us some feedback on your user experience (please).

Design notes

This is the home page of the iLab website. This page with eventual host news of up-coming workshops and events along with reports on previous activities. The three-column layout (on a large screen) could give way to two columns as and when the content suggests it. Our Twitter timeline @wsa_ilab will be a permanent feature on this page. Perhaps, when things get busier a calendar of events could go on this page or else have a page of its own.

This page will be much more visual once there's some real content to display.
